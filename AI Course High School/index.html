Why Does AI "Lie" Sometimes?

ü§î

This phenomenon is called "Hallucination".

1. Outdated or Missing Data

AI won't know about the latest viral meme if its training data is from last year.

2. Making 'Educated' Guesses

When unsure, AI tries to guess and create the most plausible-sounding answer, even if it's wrong.

3. Your Prompt is Too Vague!

Asking a very general or ambiguous question can confuse the AI, leading to inaccurate answers.

The AI Prompting Formula: P.A.C.T

P - Persona: "Act as a..."
A - Action: "Write/summarize/plan..."
C - Context: Give the AI more information, goals...
T - Tone: "Professional/Humorous/Youthful..."

Example:

"(P) Act as a nutritionist. (A) Create a 7-day meal plan for a high school student needing to boost memory. (C) The budget is low and recipes should be easy to cook. (T) Present it in a table format with a friendly tone."

Fine-Tuning the AI's "Personality"

üî• Temperature

("Creativity" Level)

Determines the randomness of the response.

  • Low (near 0): Safe, predictable, good for factual answers.
  • High (near 1): Creative, surprising, good for brainstorming, creative writing.

üéØ Top-P / Top-K

("Focus" Level)

Limits the pool of words the AI can choose from.

  • Low: Fewer word choices, results in safer, more common responses.
  • High (near 1): More word choices, allows for rarer words to appear.

üí° Pro Tip: Usually, you only need to adjust the Temperature!
